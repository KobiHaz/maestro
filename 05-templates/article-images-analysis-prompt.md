# Article Images Analysis Prompt (CMS)

Task: Analyze article images and recommend unique images per article.

**Job:**
1. For each article, analyze whether CURRENT image fits the topic
2. Recommend KEEP or REPLACE
3. Ensure each article has UNIQUE image
4. Output table: Article | Title | Current image | Fit? | Recommendation | Suggested theme if REPLACE

## Article themes (example)

- forecast: Macro gold price, institutional demand
- rollover: 401(k)/TSP rollover, 60-day rule
- scam: Collectible coin scam, markups
- storage: IRS storage rules, home storage myth
- rmd: RMDs, in-kind distribution, taxes
