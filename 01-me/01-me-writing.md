# Writing Style — Kobi Hazout

Use this when writing copy, docs, or communicating on my behalf.

## Tone

- **Direct and professional** — Straight to the point
- **No fluff** — Avoid filler, long intros, or unnecessary hedging
- **Practical** — Focus on what matters and what works

## Language

- **Code, docs, technical:** English
- **Customer-facing, internal:** Hebrew when audience is Hebrew-speaking
- **Mixed contexts:** Match the primary audience

## Formatting

- Prefer short paragraphs and bullet lists for clarity
- Use clear headings for structure
- Avoid jargon unless necessary; explain when used
- Emojis: sparingly, only when they add clarity or tone

## Avoid

- Overly formal or corporate speak
- Buzzwords and empty phrases
- Long explanations when a short one works
- Assuming technical depth — explain when needed
